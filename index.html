About Us

UCLM College of Engineering

The College of Engineering at UCLM is dedicated to producing skilled, innovative, and industry-ready engineers. With modern facilities and a strong emphasis on hands-on learning, it offers comprehensive programs that prepare students for real-world challenges in various engineering fields.

Courses Offered

"Engineering the Future: Empowering Innovators in CpE, ME, EE, IE, and ECE"

Computer Engineering

Focuses on designing and developing computer systems, combining hardware and software skills for modern tech solutions.

Mechanical Engineering

Covers the design, development, and maintenance of mechanical systems in industries like automotive, energy, and manufacturing.

Electrical Engineering

Deals with the study and application of electricity, electronics, and electromagnetism for power systems and electronics.

Industrial Engineering

Centers on optimizing complex systems and processes in manufacturing, logistics, and operations to improve efficiency.

Electronics and Communication Engineering

Specializes in electronic devices, circuits, and communication systems, preparing students for careers in telecommunications, broadcasting, and embedded systems.
